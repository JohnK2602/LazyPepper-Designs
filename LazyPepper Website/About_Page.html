Hello, I’m Eddie Moore, owner of LazyPepper Designs. We’re located in Kingstree, SC. A nice, quiet place to live, work and enjoy the beautiful surroundings that God has blessed us with. I have a wonderful family that doesn’t mind supporting my hobbies.

If you’re wondering where the name LazyPepper comes from, well it’s a simple story. I have been crafting, drawing, and woodworking for a long time. A gift my father and brother instilled in me when I was a teenager. My dad used to make Christmas gifts for our family every year and made some of the furniture in our house, including an oak banquet table for the dining room. I learned to use hand and power tools back then as well as learning to work on cars and welding too. I learned to bake and make sweets from my mom and make BBQ and chicken bog from my dad. In my twenties, our family did a little catering on the side. I continued to cook for people off and on and eventually came up with The Lazy Pepper as my catering name. I still cook and bake but now I only do it for fun.

Over the years I continued to dabble in woodworking and even built my own kitchen cabinets during our house renovations. That brings us to my woodworking business. I became fascinated with charcuterie and cutting boards and then epoxy work. The more I experimented, the more fun and interesting it became. I started selling at our town’s farmer’s market and did well enough to continue the efforts. It then became necessary to come up with a name. I always liked LazyPepper and decided to stick with it. Even though the name fits better with food, it certainly gets people’s attention, which is what a good business name should do.

I currently make charcuterie and cutting boards, noodle boards, epoxy river boards, wine barrel crafts and furniture. Although this is still not my primary occupation, I enjoy it enough toalways learn new things and try to perfect my craft. I hope you enjoy browsing our website and come back to see what new things we have from time to time.

Thank you for stopping by,

Eddie
